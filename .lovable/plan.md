
# The Glass House — Lynchburg, VA

A dark, moody, luxe single-page-feeling site (with routes) for a downtown gastro lounge by Riverfront Park. Static, no backend.

## Visual direction
- **Palette:** deep charcoal/near-black background, warm candlelit amber, brushed gold accents, ivory text
- **Typography:** elegant serif display (Playfair / Cormorant) for headlines, clean sans for body
- **Mood:** speakeasy meets gastro lounge — moody food photography, glass/reflection motifs, generous negative space, subtle scroll fade-ins

## Pages & sections

**Home (`/`)**
- Hero: full-bleed dark image, "The Glass House" wordmark, tagline ("A gastro lounge on the river — Downtown Lynchburg"), CTAs to Reservations & Menu
- Intro blurb about the concept
- Featured menu highlights (3–4 dishes/cocktails)
- "Find us" strip — Riverfront Park / downtown Lynchburg with hours
- Upcoming events teaser
- Instagram/gallery preview strip

**Menu (`/menu`)**
- Tabbed sections: Small Plates, Mains, Shareables, Desserts, Cocktails, Wine, Beer
- Elegant two-column item layout (name • price • description)
- Seasonal note at top; placeholder items you can edit later

**About (`/about`)**
- Story of the concept, the chef/bar program, the riverfront location
- Values: seasonal, local, craft

**Events & Private Bookings (`/events`)**
- Recurring nights (live music, trivia, etc. — placeholders)
- Private event/buyout info with capacity & inquiry CTA (mailto)

**Gallery (`/gallery`)**
- Masonry/grid of food, drinks, interior — lightbox on click

**Reservations (`/reservations`)**
- Static page with "Book a Table" CTA — placeholder linking out to OpenTable/Resy/phone (you choose later)
- Walk-in policy + large party note (mailto)

**Contact (`/contact`)**
- Address, phone, email, hours
- Embedded Google Map of downtown Lynchburg / Riverfront Park area
- Social links (Facebook)

## Global
- Sticky transparent-to-solid nav with logo, links, "Reserve" button
- Footer: hours, address, socials, newsletter-style sign-off (display only)
- Smooth scroll, subtle hover micro-interactions, mobile hamburger menu
- All imagery via tasteful placeholder photography (swappable later)

## Out of scope (can add later)
- Real reservation backend, newsletter capture, CMS-editable menu, online ordering
